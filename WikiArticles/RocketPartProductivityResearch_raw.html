Object description

Space Age expansion exclusive feature.

Rocket part productivity applies an additional productivity bonus to the rocket silo when crafting rocket parts.

Technology Cost Effects
1
Rocket part productivity 1
60

X 2000 × 1.5(Level)
(eg. 3000 for level 1, 4500 for level 2, 6750 for level 3)		 10% per level

Note that the 300% productivity limit does apply to this technology. It can be researched past level 30, but it will have no practical effect.

History

  • 2.0.7:
    • Introduced in Space Age expansion.
