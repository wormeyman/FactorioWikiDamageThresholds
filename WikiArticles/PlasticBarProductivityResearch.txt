Applies to the [[Plastic bar]] recipe (Chemical plant, Biochamber, and Cryogenic plant) and the [[Bioplastic]] recipe (Biochamber only). Productivity is capped at 300%; cells shown in '''bold''' indicate that the configuration has reached the cap at that level and will not improve further with additional research.
